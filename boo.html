About our products.

A Nude Eyeshadow Palette contains micas for shimmer, titanium dioxide for opacity, iron oxides for color, talc for texture, silicones as binders, and preservatives to prevent microbial growth. These ingredients create various textures and finishes for versatile makeup application.
A Pink Lipstick contains waxes for structure, oils for a smooth application, pigments like iron oxides for color, titanium dioxide for opacity, and emollients for hydration. These ingredients work together to create a creamy, vibrant product suitable for different finishes.
A Cushion contains water for hydration, emollients for a smooth texture, pigments for coverage, silicones for a lightweight feel, and UV filters for sun protection. These ingredients blend to create a lightweight, buildable coverage with a dewy finish.
We are collaborating with K-pop idols, offering formulas similar to our existing products but featuring a different range of shades. This partnership allows us to create unique and vibrant color options, appealing to fans and beauty enthusiasts alike.
